The first crucial step is to transform the compositional data: Use a log-ratio transformation, typically the centered log-ratio (clr) or isometric log-ratio (ilr) transformation. The clr transformation is often preferred for interpretability, while ilr is used for statistical properties. We will use robust CLR (RCLR) with natural log.

ps.q.df.wide.pca.tfm<-decostand(ps.q.df.wide.pca,
                                method="rclr",
                                logbase = exp)
